Bundoran - Lahinch - Clonakilty - Dingle - Enniscrone - Strandhill

BUNDORAN

September 28th - 30th 2018

This is our first trip of the year with over 70 people. For many this is the first adventure where surf club steals their heart and never gives it back. This trip includes massive 12 bed houses beside the sea, lessons with Turn'N'Surf, and a traditional Black Tie night to the exclusive Paris nightclub. We revisit Bundoran on our daytrips.

HALLOWEEN TRIP

October 26th - 29th 2018

Our legendary three night Halloween surf trip. You will be given three days to run around, surf some of the best waves in Ireland, and dress up as terrifyingly or creatively as you can for our Halloween Surf Party. We even throw in a massive gum-ball challenge where every house will duke it out for surf club glory.

DAY TRIPS

Saturday's throughout the year

For all of you who can't commit to a whole weekend away - don't fear. We run day trips throughout the year to accomodate you. We leave campus early Saturday, surf, and return for a pint in the Clubhouse after. Rentals/Lessons/Transport all included. Those whow ant to improve on a regular basis can join our surfing progression team at the beginning of the year.

REFRESHER'S TRIP

January/Febuary 2019

We start the new year off right with another large group trip. This event runs similarly to our Freshers trip, with theme nights, lessons, and 2 whole days of surf club fun.

FOREIGN TRIP

March 2019 (Fieldwork Period)

UCD Surf Club travels to sunnier shores in March. Previous destinations include Morocco, Portugal, and France. Surf Club works hard in organizing our foreign trip so that our members can become immersed in a foreign culture, experience breathtaking settings, and surf some of the best waves in the world.

END OF YEAR TRIP

May 2019

We say our final goodbye to the year in the form of a 5 day trip, from Monday to Friday after exams end. Activities include sunset surfs, welcome BBQ, theme night, hikes, and more.
